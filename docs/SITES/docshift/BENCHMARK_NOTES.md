## Useful patterns to learn

- Home grid of PDF tasks creates strong discovery.
- Tool pages need a dominant dropzone and one obvious action.
- Related tools and task chaining matter after a PDF result.
- Privacy/retention messaging is essential for documents.
- Batch, OCR, larger files, API and teams are natural paid paths.

## Do not copy

- PDF suite layouts, icons, sample documents, claims, legal/privacy copy or pricing language.

## Opportunities

- Refine PDF tool grid and dropzone states.
- Strengthen privacy and deletion messaging.
- Prepare OCR/batch/API backlog behind gates.
