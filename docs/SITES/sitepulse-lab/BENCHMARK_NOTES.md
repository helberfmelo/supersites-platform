## Useful patterns to learn

- Emergency "is it down?" intent needs one input and one answer.
- Technical reports need grades/scores plus details and recommendations.
- Redirect, headers, robots, sitemap and TTFB can be presented as tabs.
- Monitoring, history, alerts and status pages are natural paid upgrades.

## Do not copy

- Report grading systems, charts, map/status data, text, icons or third-party diagnostic claims.

## Opportunities

- Build a simple status answer followed by technical tabs.
- Add safe recommendations and related tools.
- Prepare uptime/status-page upsell without recurring probes.
